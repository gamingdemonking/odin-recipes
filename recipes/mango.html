Mango Milk Shake

Description

A silken blend of handpicked Alphonso mangoes—celebrated for their rich aroma and honeyed depth—folded into chilled organic whole milk and a touch of Madagascar vanilla. Gently whipped to a velvety texture, this indulgent shake is finished with a whisper of saffron and a delicate crown of sweet cream. Refreshing, aromatic, and decadently smooth—summer in a glass, reimagined.

Ingredients

  • 2 Mangoes
  • 3 Tbsp refined sugar
  • 2-3 glasses Milk
  • Ice

Steps

  1. Peel the Mangoes.
  2. Chop them into small pieces and put in the Blender
  3. Add 3 Tbsp refined sugar to it.
  4. Add 2-3 glasses of Milk
  5. Add Ice and blend the whole thing.
  6. Pour into glasses and serve Cold !
